WELCOME TO CHRISEN HIGH INTERNATIONAL

CHRISEN HIGH INTERNATIONAL is a senior high school established on 3rd October, 2005 by Joseph and Jennifer Dzamesi in partnership with Ray and Cam Boatright.

CHRISEN HIGH INTERNATIONAL: A Beacon of Artistic Excellence in Africa

Chrisen High International has firmly established itself as a powerhouse of artistic talent, gaining national and continental recognition for its outstanding contributions to the arts. Located in Tema, Ghana, the school has consistently nurtured young creatives, producing award-winning artists, musicians, and performers who have made a lasting impact in Africa’s art scene.

In 2023, the school’s Visual Arts Department was honored at the African Art Excellence Awards in Nairobi for its innovative approach to contemporary African art. The students' breathtaking murals and sculptures, which celebrate Ghanaian heritage and culture, have been featured in major exhibitions across Africa. Additionally,Chrisen’s drama and music ensemble have won multiple national and regional competitions, including the prestigious Tema Arts Festival, where they captivated audiences with their storytelling and musical prowess.

Beyond competitions,Chrisen High International actively promotes art education by collaborating with renowned African artists and institutions. Through these efforts, the school has become a beacon of artistic excellence, inspiring the next generation of African creatives while reinforcing Tema’s reputation as a cultural hub.
