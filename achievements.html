Welcome To Sonrise Christian High School

Sonrise Christian High is a senior high school established on 3rd October, 2005 by Joseph and Jennifer Dzamesi in partnership with Ray and Cam Boatright. Albeit Sonrise Christian High started in 2005, Sonrise Christian International school which is a basic school was established in the year 1990. Sonrise Christian High School started with 132 students and three programmes – General Science, General Arts and Business. Today, Sonrise Christian High has a student population of 700+ and five programmes. Sonrise Christian High School is affiliated to the Church of Christ.

a Brief Message from the administrator

It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to Sonrise Christian High School community. In sixteen years of providing quality education, Sonrise has grown from the grounds up to be widely recognized as the best private high school in the Volta Region and one of the best high schools in Ghana. Heavy investment has been done on infrastructures that will provide your wards with comfort and security needed for a successful education.
